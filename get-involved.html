Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about getting involved

What skills are needed to volunteer?

We welcome volunteers with diverse skills! While specific roles may require particular expertise (medical, engineering, etc.), many opportunities require only a willingness to help. Common needs include: field work assistance, administrative support, community outreach, event coordination, and translation services.

How are donations used?

90% of all donations go directly to flood relief programs including emergency supplies, shelter, medical aid, and community rebuilding. 7% covers operational costs, and 3% goes to fundraising efforts. We publish detailed annual financial reports for complete transparency.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Yes! We have remote volunteering opportunities in areas like: social media management, graphic design, content writing, data entry, research, and virtual fundraising campaigns. Indicate your interest in remote work when submitting your volunteer application.

What support do you provide to fundraisers?

We provide comprehensive support to fundraisers including: branded campaign materials, fundraising toolkits, social media promotion, guidance on effective fundraising strategies, and assistance with campaign setup on our platform. Our team is available to help you succeed in your fundraising efforts.
