Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How can I volunteer with United Against Floods?

We welcome volunteers! You can apply through our "Get Involved" page. We have opportunities for field volunteers, administrative support, and skilled professionals. All volunteers undergo a brief orientation and training.

How are donations used?

90% of all donations go directly to flood relief programs, including emergency supplies, shelter, medical aid, and community rebuilding. 7% covers operational costs, and 3% goes to fundraising efforts. We publish annual financial reports for transparency.

How can my community request assistance?

Communities affected by floods can contact our emergency hotline at +254 711 222 333 or submit a request through our website. Our assessment team will evaluate the situation and coordinate appropriate relief efforts.

Do you offer flood prevention education?

Yes! We run community workshops on flood preparedness, early warning systems, and sustainable land use. Contact our education department to schedule a workshop in your community.
